Dog

Many dogs are comforted by items that smell like their owners.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 19 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Domestic

Dogs are domesticated, quadrupedal mammals that descended from wolves. Ranging in breed, color, size, and temperaments, they are generally considered to be "man's best friend".
